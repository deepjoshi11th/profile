Welcome to the World Wide Web

A Hypertext Information Initiative

This page is a collection of pointers to information available on the World Wide Web. The web is young — but already, it connects minds across oceans.

What Happened in 1990

  • The World Wide Web — Tim Berners-Lee writes the first web browser and editor at CERN. The information age begins.
  • Hubble Space Telescope — Launched aboard the Space Shuttle Discovery. Humanity looks deeper into the cosmos.
  • German Reunification — East and West Germany are united once more. The wall has fallen.
  • The Human Genome Project — An international effort to map every human gene begins.
  • Nelson Mandela — Released from prison after 27 years. A new chapter for South Africa.
  • The Simpsons — Season 1 airs. Television will never be the same.
