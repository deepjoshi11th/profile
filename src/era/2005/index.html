🛠 Tech Revolution of 2005

+ AJAX — No More Page Reloads

Google Maps showed the world: you can update parts of a page without reloading. XMLHttpRequest changed everything.

+ YouTube — "Me at the zoo"

The first video was uploaded on April 23, 2005. 19 seconds of a guy at the San Diego Zoo. It changed media forever.

+ RSS — Subscribe to Everything

Before algorithmic feeds, people chose what to read. RSS was the open web's notification system. RIP (kind of).

+ Firefox 1.0 — The Browser Wars Return

Firefox challenged IE's monopoly. Tabbed browsing! Extensions! A web browser that didn't crash every 10 minutes!
